This is why I care.

I’m South Asian. Unfortunately, I feel as though sexual violence is a prevalent issue within my community and isn’t given enough attention. It’s mostly women and young girls who are victims of sexual violence, whether it be in marriages or familial relationships. Regardless of the scenario, it’s highly likely for perpetrators to never be reported due to the stigma around speaking up about this nature of abuse. I don’t want to blame it on South Asian culture for giving our men a superiority complex regarding gender but I hope that the following generations do a much better job to educate their children about these rights and wrongs. I hope that we as a community can learn from the unfortunate past in order to create a better and safer environment for our children.
